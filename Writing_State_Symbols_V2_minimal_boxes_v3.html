📚 Reference Guide

Terms Used in Descriptions

  • (s) SolidResidue, precipitate, deposit, crystals, powdered, ribbon, foil, wire, granules.
  • (l) LiquidMolten, liquid, water.
  • (g) GasBubbling, effervescence, fumes, gas evolved, vapour, steam.
  • (aq) AqueousAqueous, dilute, solution, acids, alkalis, dissolved in water.

Important Note

Soluble substances do not automatically get (aq) unless they are dissolved in water.

For example, solid sodium chloride on its own is still NaCl(s), even though it is soluble in water.
